Video Game Information

Minecraft

Minecraft is a game about placing blocks and going on adventures. You play this game most of the time with our cousins down the block. You have created a nice house and a big farm as part of your adventure in this world.

Killer Instinct

Killer Instinct is a series of fighting video games. You enjoy to play this with me to fight against me and my favorite character Jago. You sometimes play this alone in order to get some practice to beat me one day

The Binding Of Isaac

The Binding of Isaac is a 3d rogue like game seen from a top angle view. You love to play this game alone and show me what you have unlocked and how far you have gotten into the game itself.

Call of Duty: Black Ops 2

Call of Duty: Black Ops 2 is a first person shooter that takes place in the future. You love to play this game with me or alone as it has many things for you to unlock. Sometimes you wish you could get as good as I am when I play this game
